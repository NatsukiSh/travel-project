Why Monschau?

Monschau tourist destination No. 1 in the Eifel

Monschau is unique in many ways. The historic old town in the narrow valley of the Rur is reminiscent of the days of cloth makers in the 17th century. Well-preserved houses in the half-timbered style stand right next to the manorial houses of the cloth makers. With the many different sights, Monschau in the Eifel has something to offer at any time of the year!

The villages around Monschau are also typical of the Eifel, whether it's the Venndörfer on the edge of the Hohes Venn high moor area - such as Kalterherberg, Konzen and Mützenich - or the federal gold village of Höfen with its hedgerow landscape on the edge of the Eifel National Park.
